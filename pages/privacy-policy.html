Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Attendyn collects and stores attendance data, subject information, and user preferences securely on our servers. We value your privacy and do not share your personal information with third parties.

Data Usage

Your attendance data is used solely to provide you with attendance tracking, analytics, and predictions to help you stay on track with your academic goals.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our cloud database to ensure synchronization across your devices. We maintain modern server infrastructure to protect your personal attendance information.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. Your privacy and data integrity are our top priority.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app.
